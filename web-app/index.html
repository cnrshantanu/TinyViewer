Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who just want it to work — no config files, no VPN, no router setup required.

🌍
Access from anywhere
One click creates a secure Cloudflare tunnel. Works behind any firewall — no port forwarding needed.
⚡
Low latency
Adaptive JPEG quality automatically adjusts to your connection speed to keep the stream smooth.
🖱
Full mouse & keyboard
Click, drag, scroll, type — including Cmd, Ctrl, Fn, and any modifier combo. Feels like sitting at the Mac.
⌨
Built-in terminal
One click opens a full interactive shell in your browser. Run commands, manage files, restart services remotely.
🔒
Secure by default
Firebase auth + one-time session tokens + HTTPS via Cloudflare. Only you can access your Mac.
🆓
Free forever
No subscriptions, no caps, no paywalls. Self-hosted means your screen and data never touch our servers.

How it works

Up and running in minutes.

No port forwarding, no SSH keys, no DevOps knowledge required.

1
Install Tiny Viewer on your Mac
Download the free app, open it, and sign in with your Google account. The menu-bar icon confirms it's running.
2
Start a session
Click Start in the app. A unique Cloudflare URL is generated automatically — no setup required.
3
Connect from any browser
Open this page, click Access My Mac, and sign in with Google. Your Mac's screen appears instantly.
4
Work remotely
Control your Mac fully — mouse, keyboard, apps. Open the built-in terminal for CLI access. Adjust quality for slower connections.
